### **Problem Analysis:**

**Current Broken Behavior:**
1. **First PDCA in version 0.3.16.0** (`2025-10-24-UTC-1954.pdca.md`):
   - Shows: `**🔗 Previous PDCA:** N/A (First in chain)`
   - Should link to: Last PDCA of 0.3.15.1 (`2025-10-24-UTC-1531.pdca.md`)

2. **Last PDCA in version 0.3.16.0** (`2025-11-10-UTC-0945.pdca.md`):
   - Shows: Links to **itself** (self-referential loop!)
   - Should show: `**➡️ Next PDCA:** N/A (Last in chain)` or blank

**Root Cause:**
- `updateChainLinksInternal()` only looks for PDCAs **within the same session directory**
- No logic to find previous/next version's session directory
- No logic to detect "last in version" vs "last in chain overall"
