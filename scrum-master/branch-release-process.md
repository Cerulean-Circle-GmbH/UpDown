# Branch Management and Release Process in UpDown Scrum

## Overview
This document formalizes the process for branch management, merging, and release in the UpDown project, as revealed by the PO and Scrum Master.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Product Owner (PO):**
  - Collects all feature branches and completed work from other roles.
  - Decides what gets merged into the main branch.
  - Guides and executes the merging process.
  - Checks quality with the Tester for all deliverables before merging.
- **Tester:**
  - Assists PO in quality checks of deliverables before merging.
- **Developers/Other Roles:**
  - Complete tasks in feature branches and notify PO when ready for review/merge.

## Process Steps
1. **Branch Completion:**
   - When a task is done, it exists as a feature branch.
2. **PO Review:**
   - PO collects all branches and reviews deliverables.
3. **Quality Assurance:**
   - PO and Tester check quality and completeness of deliverables.
4. **Merge to Main:**
   - PO guides and executes the merge of approved branches into the main branch.
5. **Release:**
   - This process is called the release process and is part of daily business whenever a task is completed.

## Notes
- The process is relaxed outside of sprints during QA audits or branch management.
- Sprint methodology resumes after restart or at the beginning of a new sprint.
